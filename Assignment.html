The Harmonic Minor Mode
Frequently heard in classical, pop, and metal.

Utilizing the natural 7th rather than the b7th (as in Aeolian), leads to a more sinister, almost cemetary type sound

Scale formula:

1, 2, b3, 4, 5, b6, 7

Best used over a resolving and corresponding 5th chord (maj not min)
